About Me

I was born and raised in Miami, FL. I've been active my whole life and that followed me during my time at UCF where I was a personal trainer and exercise physiologist at Orlando Health in their Cardiac Rehab unit. I recieved my Bachelors of Science in Kinesiology, and worked as a Wellness Coordinator managing the fitness department of a gym in Dr. Phillips. I was drawn to development by my love of learning, and I'm excited to see where it takes me! In my free time you would find me hanging out with friends and family, working out, or walking with my dog and husband in downtown Orlando.
